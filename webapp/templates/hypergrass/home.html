Hyper4Grass

Hyper4Grass provides ultra-precise information for the monitoring of grass traits such as protein and fiber content. Our service for collecting high resolution in the field is supported by a combination of sensors that collect RGB, near-infrared, and hyperspectral data in a single flight. Our information service helps farmers to determine the optimal time for mowing.
